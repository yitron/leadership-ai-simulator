## Findings

### Finding 1: Performance Gap (Handshaw Stage 1)
- **Current state**: Leaders in corporate organisations are not effectively managing or leveraging AI-enabled teams. They delegate AI tasks without understanding outputs, fail to set AI usage norms, and struggle with trust and accountability when AI is involved in decisions.
- **Gap**: Leaders lack practical skills to integrate AI into team workflows, evaluate AI outputs critically, and lead with both human and AI contributors.
- **Priority**: P0
- **Impact**: Teams underperform; AI investment not realised; risk of poor decisions from unreviewed AI outputs

### Finding 2: Instructional Format
- **Current state**: No existing simulator/chatbot prototype exists
- **Gap**: Need an AI-powered scenario simulator with real corporate situations where users make leadership decisions with AI context
- **Priority**: P0
- **Impact**: Without hands-on practice, training reverts to passive knowledge transfer (the same mistake as the LMS case study)

### Finding 3: Scenario Content
- **Current state**: Generic leadership training does not include AI-specific dilemmas
- **Gap**: Need 3–5 realistic corporate scenarios covering: AI in performance reviews, delegating to AI tools, managing team resistance to AI, AI-assisted hiring decisions
- **Priority**: P1
- **Impact**: Without real-world relevance, learners disengage

### Finding 4: Assessment & Feedback
- **Current state**: No mechanism to assess quality of leadership decisions in AI contexts
- **Gap**: Simulator must provide immediate, specific feedback tied to performance objectives
- **Priority**: P1
- **Impact**: Without feedback loops, learners cannot self-correct

### Finding 5: Evaluation Plan (Handshaw Stage 6)
- **Current state**: No evaluation rubric defined
- **Gap**: Need Kirkpatrick Level 1 (reaction) + Level 3 (behaviour change) measurement
- **Priority**: P2
- **Impact**: Cannot measure ROI or course effectiveness

|-----|----------|------------------------|

| Immediate AI-generated feedback per decision | P1 | No self-correction; shallow learning |
| Progress tracking / score | P1 | Cannot measure learner performance |
| Evaluation rubric (L1 + L3) | P2 | Cannot measure course impact |
